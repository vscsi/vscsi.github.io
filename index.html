The Next Step

Born and raised in Hong Kong, have been living here for the most of my life. Went overseas to study for a few years. It was a great experience. Since I was young, my parents invested a great deal on me to learn music. As I grew up, it seemed only logical to take up a music related career. After my education, I did free-lance music production, and I started giving private music lessons.

Admist a pandemic outbreak, and such uncertain times. I decided I needed to get out of my comfort zone and try to be something I've always seen myself doing. That is why I chose to embark on a journey which brings me to a whole new territory. Learning new skills and hoping to get a change of scenary and widen my horizons.
